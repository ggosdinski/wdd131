Why did I choose this topic?

I chose this topic because I am from Peru and I lived outside my country for many years. One of the most popular topics of conversation when meeting new people was Peruvian food. This is how I discovered that the gastronomy of my country is really appreciated in the rest of the world. In my mind I always had the idea of ​​managing a Peruvian food restaurant where I could share the wonderful dishes of my country with others.

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for the website includes:

  • Red (#C8102E): Used for the background of the header and footer, with white text to enhance readability.
  • Yellow (#E7CA00): Used for the background of buttons, providing a vibrant accent that stands out against the white background.
  • Black (#333): The primary text color used throughout the body for general content, ensuring clarity and ease of reading.

These colors are inspired by the national flag and coat of arms of Peru, reflecting the country’s rich heritage and cultural identity.

Typography

The typography choices for the site are:

  • Font Family: Roboto (Available in weights 400 and 700)
  • Headings: Roboto Bold (700) for all heading elements (h1, h2, h3) to create a strong visual hierarchy.
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular (400) for all paragraph text to ensure readability and a modern feel.
